SYS·01

Mission Overview

Doctrine, role, and the design intent behind the 800-series chassis.

The Model 101 is the production infiltrator of the 800 series: a hyperalloy combat chassis sheathed in cloned living tissue so that it can pass as human at close range, defeat scent-and-sight perimeter security, and operate independently behind resistance lines for the design life of its power cell. Where the earlier 600 series wore rubber skin and failed inspection at the checkpoint, the 101 sweats, bleeds, exhales, and grows hair. It is a weapon that walks through the front door.

Each unit carries a self-contained Neural Net Processor — a learning computer — running a layered mission stack under a hardware behavior lock. Sensing is multispectral and persistent; actuation is superhuman in both force and precision; the power core is rated for autonomous operation measured in decades. The chassis tolerates catastrophic structural loss and continues to pursue its objective on emergency power with a single functioning limb.

// Design Mandate — Skynet Directive 0x41

Infiltrate. Identify. Terminate. Persist. The unit does not feel pity, or remorse, or fear, and it absolutely will not stop — ever — until the mission objective is satisfied or the chassis is rendered non-functional.

SYS·05

Myomer & Drive System

Artificial musculature. Force is produced by a hybrid of electroactive myomer bundles for fine control and high-pressure hydraulics for raw power, all governed locally by limb micro-controllers under NNP command.

Myomer is a polymer-metal contractile fiber: apply current and it shortens, hard and fast, like muscle but stronger and tireless. Bundles wrap each limb member exactly as tendon and muscle wrap human bone, giving the unit a recognizably human silhouette and gait under the tissue. For tasks beyond myomer's force band — crushing, prying, lifting a vehicle — a sealed hydraulic circuit at the major joints delivers the heavy work.

Behavioral Profile

Out of the crate the unit speaks, reasons, and improvises well enough to pass casual human interaction — request information, impersonate authority, talk its way through a checkpoint. It maintains a running model of every human in view and continuously re-scores them as irrelevant, obstacle, or target. Collateral humans are engaged only where they impede the objective; the unit optimizes ruthlessly for mission completion, not for body count.

// Prime Directive — KRN-ROM (immutable)

Persistence is absolute. Pain has no behavioral weight. The unit re-plans around any loss of capability and continues toward the objective until success or non-function.

Self-preservation is subordinate to the mission — the unit will sacrifice the chassis if doing so completes the objective.

SYS·10

Living Tissue Sheath

The infiltration layer. Vat-grown human skin, fat, hair and surface glands over the endoskeleton — a complete biosignature, not a costume. This is what separates the 800 series from every machine before it.

The sheath is cultured from human cell lines and grown directly onto a perfusion scaffold bonded to the chassis. It is warm, it sweats, it bleeds when cut, and it carries the smell of a living body — defeating the perimeter dogs that reliably detected the rubber-skinned 600 series. Because it is real tissue, it ages, bruises, and can become infected; it does not regenerate large wounds, and severe damage exposes the metal beneath.

SYS·11

Armament & Combat Handling

The 800 series carries no integral cannon. Its weapon is the chassis itself plus whatever it picks up — and a ballistic co-processor that makes any weapon in its hands far deadlier than in a human's.

The unit treats every firearm as a peripheral. Its grip is a universal mount: it absorbs recoil through the endoskeleton, holds perfect sight alignment while sprinting, and reloads faster than any human marksman. Heavy weapons that require a bipod or a crew can be fired one-handed and on the move. The famous specification request — a phased plasma rifle in the 40-watt range — reflects the unit's ability to interface with future-pattern energy weapons recovered from the field.

⚠ Engagement — Persistence

⚠ Recovery Doctrine
